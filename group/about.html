About Us - Meet the Team

Lauren

Lauren is an inveterate globetrotter, having traveled the world to capture our clear, colorful images of gorgeous blossoms. Her travels have taken her to New Zealand, Fiji, Bali, British Columbia, England, France, and Hawaii—to name just a few.

Rashaad

Rashaad's strategic insights are only matched by his technical know-how. He'll often go from negotiating with our manufacturers to diving into the nitty-gritty of cropping photos. He's our resident jack-of-all-trades and the only one who knows how to work the epresso machine.

Teresa

Is a gardener and has often wondered how best to capture these natural images and share them with as many people as possible. Perhaps her green thumb is why she's also such an amazing customer experience rep. Nurture is in her nature!

Henry

Henry isn't your typical designer/developer, he's a master of color and light. He has worked tirelessly with our team to create our site and serves as web master extraordinaire. In his free time, Henry loves to bike around the city finding and creating adventure where he goes.

Jack (is a girl)

Jack has an eye for detail and an amazing ability to work with all kinds of people from our manufacturers to our warehouse team to our partners and vendors. She puts the dream in dream team. But, we'll admit, we also just kind of dig her accent.
